Map & Graph search layouts

Compare four ways to add multiple objects without leaving the canvas. Open Map or Graph, then try adding several objects in one session.

  • Baseline
    Modal (current)

    Full-screen dialog. Closes and clears search after each add.

  • A
    Expandable strip

    Compact search bar on the map; click or press ▾ to expand results.

  • B
    Side dock

    Left panel for search and results; collapse to a slim rail when idle.

  • C
    Floating dropdown

    Search button in the top-left; opens a floating panel over the map.
